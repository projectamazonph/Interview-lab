## 5.12 "Trick" or Red-Flag Questions (12 questions)

These questions test honesty and judgment.

1. Have you personally managed large Amazon PPC budgets?
2. Can you guarantee lower ACoS in 30 days?
3. Would you ever change bids without documenting it?
4. Is high search volume always good?
5. Should you always pause keywords with no sales?
6. Should you always increase budget when sales increase?
7. Is low ACoS always better?
8. Can you copy competitor listings?
9. Should you use competitor brand names in every campaign?
10. Can you promise first-page organic ranking?
11. Is PPC the only reason a product does not sell?
12. Should you scale ads if inventory is low?

Strong candidates should mention context, data volume, listing quality, inventory, profitability, and SOP approval.
