# Amazon VA Interview Lab - Product Vision

## Product Name
Amazon VA Interview Lab

## Vision
Amazon VA Interview Lab is a web app that prepares candidates for Amazon marketplace VA roles through:
1. Role-based interview question banks
2. AI mock interviews
3. Resume and cover letter coaching
4. Downloadable templates
5. Practical Amazon PPC/data exercises
6. Portfolio project generators
7. Employer-style scoring rubrics
8. Learning paths based on the user's target role
9. Agent-assisted coaching with strict truthfulness and anti-fabrication guardrails

## Target Roles
| Role | What the app trains for |
|------|------------------------|
| Amazon PPC VA | Campaign setup, keyword research, ACoS/ROAS, bid adjustments, reporting |
| Amazon Account VA | Seller Central tasks, catalog support, inventory checks, case logs |
| Amazon Listing VA | Titles, bullets, A+ content, indexing, keyword placement |
| Amazon Reporting VA | Search term reports, campaign reports, KPI summaries |
| Amazon Agency Assistant | SOP execution, ClickUp tasks, client updates, changelogs |
| Senior PPC Assistant | Audit support, optimization logic, launch sequencing, campaign QA |

## PPC Focus
The app should be especially strong for PPC roles because reference docs already define rich PPC workflows, including CPC ceiling formulas, keyword promotion/negation rules, ACoS thresholds, campaign launch stages, and reporting requirements.

## Recommended Name
Amazon VA Interview Lab because it is broad enough for future roles.

### Primary CTA:

### Secondary CTA:

## Real Differentiator
The killer feature is the combination of:
1. Interview coaching
2. Resume/cover letter generation
3. Realistic Amazon PPC practical tests
4. Downloadable job-search assets
5. Truthfulness guardrails

Most interview prep apps are generic. This one is highly specific to Amazon VA hiring because it teaches candidates how to talk about real workflows: campaign launch sequencing, search term reports, listing readiness, ACoS/ROAS, keyword harvesting, negative keywords, budget pacing, reporting cadence, and SOP execution.
