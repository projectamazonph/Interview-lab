# Amazon VA Interview Lab - PRD

## Problem Statement
Many Amazon VA candidates apply to roles with weak resumes, generic cover letters, shallow marketplace knowledge, and little ability to explain real PPC or Seller Central workflows. Employers increasingly want candidates who can do more than say "I know Amazon PPC." They want proof that the candidate understands campaign structure, keyword research, reporting, optimization rules, client communication, and SOP discipline.

This app helps candidates prepare for interviews and practical tests by turning Amazon operations knowledge into guided practice, scored mock interviews, resume improvements, cover letter drafts, and downloadable job-search assets.

## Target Users

### Primary: Entry-level Amazon VA candidate
They may have general VA experience but limited Amazon-specific knowledge. They need role explanations, beginner guides, sample answers, resume repositioning, and confidence-building.

### Secondary: Intermediate Amazon PPC assistant
They know ACoS, ROAS, broad/exact campaigns, and Seller Central basics, but they need structured interview practice, stronger answers, and practical case studies.

### Tertiary: Agency trainee or junior media buyer
They need a more advanced path covering campaign launch prioritization, keyword harvesting, negative keyword logic, budget pacing, reporting, and account audit workflows.

### Admin: Course owner/content manager
They need to add questions, edit rubrics, upload templates, manage role packs, and approve AI-generated content before it is published.

## Goals
| Goal | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Improve interview readiness | Help users answer behavioral, technical, and scenario questions clearly |
| Improve job application quality | Generate truthful, tailored resumes and cover letters |
| Teach Amazon-specific language | Help candidates use terms like ACoS, ROAS, CVR, CPC, STR, indexing, ranking, PPC stages |
| Simulate real hiring screens | Include mock interviews, timed tests, and practical exercises |
| Build candidate portfolios | Generate sample audit summaries, campaign plans, keyword logs, and reporting samples |
| Reduce generic VA applications | Make candidate materials specific to Amazon marketplace work |

## Non-goals
| Non-goal | Reason |
|----------|--------|
| Guarantee job placement | Risky, misleading claim |
| Write fake experience | Must maintain candidate integrity |
| Encourage candidates to lie about tools | They can say "familiar with" only if trained; otherwise "currently learning" |
| Expose proprietary internal playbooks verbatim | Training content should be transformed into candidate-facing lessons |
| Replace full Amazon Ads certification | It is interview prep and job readiness, not an official certification |
| Scrape job boards without permission | Use user-provided job descriptions or approved integrations |
